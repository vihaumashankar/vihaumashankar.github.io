about

Our mission is to make searching for modern home design inspiration easier. With collections of photos, Instagram connections, as well as coordinating patterns for you to use, we strive to make your experience with interior design a breeze. Our love for design led up to the foundations of this website. As only a team of three, we designed, built, and implemented this site in under two weeks. We focused on coordination and aesthetics, convenience, and most importantly, functionality. Not only do we have a small collection of furniture to buy, but inspirations on how to incorporate it into your room. Don't find what you need, contact us too! But most importantly, get designing!
